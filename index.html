Petz Park

Welcome to Petz Park, a webring themed around the P.F. Magic Petz game. Each day, a new pet will visit your corner of the park, and in turn, your pet will wander over to other people's sites. You are also welcome to post the widget on your page without submitting a pet, to add a little taste of 90s era virtual petz to your page.
